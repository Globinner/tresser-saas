Welcome to the Tresser Referral Program! This guide explains how to earn 
commissions by referring new barbershops to Tresser.

================================================================================
                         HOW THE PROGRAM WORKS
================================================================================

1. You get a unique REFERRAL CODE (example: MIKE25)
2. You share this code with barbershop owners
3. When they sign up using your code and pay for a subscription
4. You earn a commission (percentage of their payment)

================================================================================
                          HOW TO SHARE YOUR CODE
================================================================================

OPTION 1: DIRECT LINK (BEST)
-------------------------------
Give potential customers this link:

    https://tresser.app/auth/sign-up?ref=YOUR_CODE

Example: https://tresser.app/auth/sign-up?ref=MIKE25

When they click this link, your code is automatically applied!


OPTION 2: TELL THEM THE CODE
-------------------------------
Tell them:
"When you sign up at tresser.app, enter referral code: YOUR_CODE"

They will see a "Referral Code" field during sign-up where they type your code.

PRICING:

================================================================================
                          HOW YOU GET PAID
================================================================================

1. EARNING:
   - You earn commission on EVERY payment they make
   - Commission is calculated automatically
   - Example: If they pay $99/month and you get 25% = $24.75 per month

2. DURATION:
   - You earn for the duration specified in your agreement
   - Example: 12 months means you earn on their first 12 payments
   - Some codes are "lifetime" = you earn forever on that customer

3. TRACKING:
   - Tresser tracks all your referrals automatically
   - You can request a report of your earnings anytime

4. PAYOUTS:
   - Payouts are processed [monthly / upon request]
   - Minimum payout: $[amount]
   - Payment methods: PayPal, bank transfer

================================================================================
                             TIPS FOR SUCCESS
================================================================================

1. TARGET THE RIGHT PEOPLE:
   - Barbershop owners
   - Salon owners
   - Barber school graduates opening their first shop
   - Shops currently using paper or basic methods

2. HIGHLIGHT PAIN POINTS:
   - "Tired of double-bookings?"
   - "Losing track of client preferences?"
   - "Want clients to book online?"
   - "Need to track team performance?"

3. OFFER TO HELP:
   - Offer to walk them through the sign-up
   - Show them the demo at tresser.app/demo
   - Answer basic questions

4. FOLLOW UP:
   - If they showed interest but didn't sign up, follow up!
   - Ask if they have questions
   - Remind them of the free trial
