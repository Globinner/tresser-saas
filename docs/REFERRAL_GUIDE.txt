=====================================================
       TRESSER REFERRAL SYSTEM - STEP BY STEP GUIDE
=====================================================

This guide explains how to create and manage referral codes
for marketers, resellers, and affiliates.


=====================================================
STEP 1: ACCESS THE ADMIN PANEL
=====================================================

1. Log in to Tresser with your admin email (globinner@gmail.com)
2. In the dashboard sidebar, click "Admin Panel" at the bottom
3. This takes you to /admin/referrals


=====================================================
STEP 2: CREATE A REFERRAL CODE
=====================================================

1. Click "Create New Code" button
2. Fill in the details:
   
   - CODE: Short memorable code (e.g., "MIKE25", "SARAH30")
     * Will be auto-capitalized
     * Keep it short and easy to remember
   
   - OWNER NAME: The marketer/reseller's full name
     * Example: "Mike Johnson"
   
   - OWNER EMAIL: Their email address
     * Used for communication and payout tracking
   
   - COMMISSION PERCENT: How much they earn per sale
     * Example: 25 = they get 25% of each payment
     * Typical range: 10-30%
   
   - DURATION (MONTHS): How long they earn commission
     * Example: 12 = they earn for 12 months per customer
     * Leave empty = lifetime (they earn forever)
     * Recommended: 12 months

3. Click "Create Code"


=====================================================
STEP 3: SHARE THE REFERRAL LINK
=====================================================

Each code has a unique signup link:

   https://tresser.com/auth/sign-up?ref=CODE

Example for code "MIKE25":
   https://tresser.com/auth/sign-up?ref=MIKE25

How to share:
1. In the admin panel, click "Copy Link" next to the code
2. Send this link to your marketer/reseller
3. They share it with potential customers

Alternative:
- Customers can also enter the code manually during signup
- There's a "Referral Code (optional)" field on the signup page


=====================================================
STEP 4: TRACK SIGNUPS & EARNINGS
=====================================================

In the Admin Panel, you can see:

- SIGNUPS: How many customers signed up with each code
- EARNED: Total commission earned by the referrer
- PAID: How much you've already paid them
- OWED: How much you still owe them (Earned - Paid)

Click "View Commissions" to see individual transactions.


=====================================================
STEP 5: PAY YOUR REFERRERS
=====================================================

When it's time to pay a referrer:

1. Calculate how much you owe (check "Owed" column)
2. Pay them via your preferred method:
   - PayPal
   - Bank transfer
   - Check
   - Cash
3. Record the payout in the system:
   - Click "Record Payout" next to their code
   - Enter amount paid
   - Select payment method
   - Add reference number (transaction ID, check number, etc.)
4. Click "Record"

This updates their "Paid" total and reduces "Owed".


=====================================================
HOW COMMISSIONS WORK
=====================================================

1. Customer signs up with referral code "MIKE25"
2. Customer subscribes and pays $99/month
3. System calculates: $99 × 25% = $24.75
4. Commission is recorded automatically
5. Mike's "Earned" increases by $24.75
6. This repeats every month (until duration limit)

IMPORTANT: 
- PayPal pays YOU the full $99
- YOU pay Mike separately (not automatic)
- The system only TRACKS what you owe


=====================================================
MANAGING CODES
=====================================================

DISABLE A CODE:
- Click the toggle switch to deactivate
- Code still exists but won't accept new signups
- Existing referrals continue earning

DELETE A CODE:
- Not recommended (breaks history)
- Instead, just disable it


=====================================================
RECOMMENDED COMMISSION STRUCTURE
=====================================================

For most SaaS businesses:
- Commission: 20-25%
- Duration: 12 months

Examples:
- Standard Partner: 20% for 12 months
- Premium Partner: 25% for 12 months  
- VIP Partner: 30% for 24 months

This motivates referrers while protecting your margins.


=====================================================
EXAMPLE SCENARIO
=====================================================

1. You create code "SARAH25" for Sarah (25%, 12 months)

2. Sarah shares her link with a barbershop owner

3. Barbershop signs up and subscribes at $99/month

4. Each month for 12 months:
   - Customer pays: $99
   - Sarah earns: $24.75
   - You keep: $74.25

5. After month 12:
   - Sarah stops earning from this customer
   - You keep full $99

6. Sarah's total earning from this customer: $297
